Essential Components:
Players: Represent players with attributes like hand, field, deck, trash, life, and DON!! energy.
Cards: Include basic attributes (name, effects, cost, etc.) and a way to execute their effects.
Game State: A global representation of the current game state, including:
    Player information (hand size, field cards, life points, etc.)
    Current turn and phase (e.g., draw phase, main phase, battle phase)
    DON!! energy levels for each player
    Win/loss conditions
Actions: Define possible actions for the AI:
    Draw a card
    Play a card
    Activate a card's effect
    Attack with a character
    Use DON!! cards
    End the turn
